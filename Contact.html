About Us

StreEats LLC is a Filipino company that specializes in the production of food products and services.
StreEats has been operating since 1998 when it was founded by two brothers who were both in the same industry.
It started out as a small business, but now has grown into one of the largest companies in the Philippines. We are proud to be part of this great family!
